Expereince Builder

Pros:

  • Easy to use with a lot of options
  • Has a search bar that connects to the map and can highlight the selected record
  • Can have lists showing records that are connected to the map data
  • Has the option for a navigational widget
  • Once there is a workflow it is simple to rebuild an Experience Builder

Cons:

  • Actions and triggers can be confusing and the wrong selection can mess with the data
  • Customization is limited and the style of widget items is not as customizable as potentially needed
